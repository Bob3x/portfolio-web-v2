About Me

I’m Borislav Ginov, a full-stack developer with a background in art & design. Originally from Plovdiv, Bulgaria, I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Interior Design and a Master’s in Digital Arts. After moving to Berlin, I gained professional experience in 3D architecture visualization and product rendering, where I honed my skills in design, teamwork, and project conceptualization.
Driven by curiosity and a desire to grow, I transitioned into web development, where I discovered a deep interest in building functional and visually appealing digital solutions. Now, as a full-stack developer, I’m excited to bring my unique blend of design expertise and technical skills to innovative projects.
